A Data-Driven Deep Dive into X100V & X100VI Aesthetics

We analyzed over 240 film simulation recipes to uncover the "Community Standard" for Fujifilm photography. Here is what the data says about how photographers are styling their images in 2026.

1. The Favorite Look

Classic Chrome and Classic Negative are the undisputed kings of the Fuji ecosystem. They account for the vast majority of all recipes.

2. Color Grading Trends

The community is obsessed with Warmth. The data shows a massive cluster of recipes shifting White Balance towards Red (Magenta) and Yellow (negative Blue), creating that signature "vintage" look.

3. Dynamic Range

DR400 is the standard. Users prioritize preserving highlight information to achieve a soft roll-off, essential for a film-like aesthetic.
